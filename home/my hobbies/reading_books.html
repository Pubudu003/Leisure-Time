READING BOOKS

Reading books is a wonderful hobby that can provide a wide range of benefits. Not only is it a great way to escape from the stresses of daily life and unwind, but it can also help to improve vocabulary, critical thinking skills, and mental agility. Through reading, we can explore new worlds, learn about different cultures and perspectives, and gain new insights and knowledge. Moreover, reading can be a very personal and intimate experience, allowing us to connect with characters and stories in a way that can be deeply emotional and fulfilling. Overall, book reading is a timeless hobby that can bring immense joy and enrichment to our lives.

Reading books is one of my favorite hobby among other hobbies. I reserve three-four days per week and two-three hours per day to reading books.
